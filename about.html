What We Offer

LCAFE Esports is a community-driven esports hub dedicated to empowering youth through competitive gaming, technology, teamwork, and digital skills development.

Mission

Our mission is to create opportunities for aspiring gamers, content creators, and future technology leaders by providing structured esports experiences in local communities.To become one of South Africa's leading grassroots esports organizations, building pathways for young gamers to compete, learn, grow, and discover opportunities in gaming, technology, and digital innovation.

Vision

We encourage young people to explore technology, streaming, content creation, event management, and esports-related careers. We collaborate with schools, youth groups, and community organizations to create safe and engaging esports experiences.

Pop-Up Gaming Events

Our signature pop-up tournaments bring competitive gaming directly into communities, schools, and public venues, creating exciting opportunities for players to compete and connect.

Esports Tournament

Regular competitive tournaments featuring EA FC, Street Fighter, Minecraft, and other popular titles with prizes, rankings, and community recognition.

Values

We believe gaming is more than entertainment. Esports develops strategic thinking, communication, leadership, discipline, and problem-solving skills that can benefit young people both inside and outside the gaming world.
