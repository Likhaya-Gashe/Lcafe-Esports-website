🕹️ About Us

LCAFE Esports is a youth-focused initiative committed to unlocking gaming potential in township communities. We operate as a pop-up esports company, currently hosting tournaments at selected schools and malls, with a long-term vision of securing a permanent venue inside a local mall.

🌟 Our Mission


Is to create safe, inclusive, and inspiring spaces where young people can:

  • 🏆 Compete in organized esports tournaments
  • 💻 Discover digital skills
  • 🌐 Explore careers in competitive gaming, content creation, and web development

🎯 Our Vision


We aim to introduce esports to both primary and high schools, cultivating talent and digital literacy from an early age. Through:

  • 🎮School-based tournaments.
  • 📚Community workshops.
  • 🧠Mentorship programs.
We empower the next generation of gamers, creators, and tech innovators.

💼 Funding & Growth


We are open to investments and funding partnerships to help expand our operations. Our goal is to secure a permanent esports hub inside a nearby shopping mall — a space that can serve as a digital playground for youth across the region.

We welcome collaboration from:

  • 🏢 Private investors.
  • 🤝 Corporate sponsors.
  • 🏫 Educational institutions.
  • 🏛️ Local government initiatives.
